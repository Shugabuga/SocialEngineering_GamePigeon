Careful, buddy!

What you just clicked on wasn't a game of pool, but a link. iOS 10 converts links into nice pretty bubbles, but this can be used maliciously.

Next time I would confirm if there was a link attached to the bottom of this message bubble. If there's no subtitle with a URL, you are likely safe, but only play with friends and family, and not strangers. You never know who may be trying to hack or decieve you!
